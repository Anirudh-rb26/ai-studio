# AI Usage Disclosure

This project was developed using a combination of human effort and AI-assisted tools.

- The frontend was developed mostly by a human developer with minimal AI assistance (approximately 95% human, 5% AI).
- The backend development was a mix of human and AI contributions, about 50% human and 50% AI.
- All tests were generated entirely using AI tools (100% AI).
- AI tools were primarily used to generate test cases, assist with backend code, and streamline development tasks.
- Final code and tests were reviewed, edited, and integrated manually to ensure correctness and quality.
- AI was used responsibly, with appropriate oversight during all stages of development.

This disclosure is provided to maintain transparency regarding the use of AI in this project, as per the submission requirements.
